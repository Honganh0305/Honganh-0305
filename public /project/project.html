Pig Game

The Pig Game is a simple yet strategic dice game where two players compete to be the first to reach a target score, typically 100 points. Players take turns rolling a single dice, accumulating points based on the roll results. They can keep rolling to increase their score, but if they roll a 1, their accumulated points for that round are lost, and their turn ends. Players can also choose to "hold" their score, adding the round points to their total and passing the dice to the other player. The game combines luck and decision-making, making it exciting and unpredictable.
